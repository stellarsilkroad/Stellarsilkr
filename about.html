Stellar Silk Road (Tangshan) International Trade Co., Ltd.

Located in Tangshan, China, Stellar Silk Road International Trade Co., Ltd. is a comprehensive company focusing on global trade services. Our main business sectors include solar electrical appliances, steel products, footwear & apparel, pet supplies, packaging products, and cycling accessories. Based on China’s strong industrial foundation and the Belt and Road initiative as a bridge, we connect every corner of the world, delivering high-quality and affordable Chinese industrial goods worldwide.

Advantages

Our company is located in Tangshan City, Hebei Province, China's first provincial economy. In 2024, the city's GDP exceeded one trillion yuan. Tangshan enjoys abundant regional advantages and convenient transportation, situated within the one-hour economic circle of the capital and closely cooperating with the capital’s economy.

Tangshan boasts convenient transport and import/export conditions, including major ports such as Jingtang Port and Caofeidian Port. By 2025, Tangshan Port Group’s cargo throughput ranks second in the world.

Tangshan has a strong industrial base, with steel production ranking among the world's top. Its industrial products and steel derivatives have absolute advantages.

History

The history of Tangshan traces back to 645 AD during the Tang dynasty, named after a mountain following Emperor Li Shimin’s eastern expedition. Over more than a thousand years of development, it has become one of the key cities in northern China.
