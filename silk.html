Silk Quilt Introduction

Over two thousand years ago, Chinese silk traveled along the Silk Road to the world, becoming a currency-like equivalent, a symbol of nobility and quality.

Today, we continue this honor, using pure natural silk to create cloud-like silk quilts.

The origin of silk quilts dates back five thousand years. It is not just a quilt but a gift from China to the world over millennia.

From the ancient Silk Road to modern civilization, silk has always been the world's top natural textile. We insist on using 100% natural silk to make our quilts.

Why Choose Our Silk Quilts?

  • Pure natural animal protein fiber, compatible with the human body.
  • Respect for life and gentle processing.
  • Natural biodegradability, environmentally friendly with no pollution.
  • Thermal comfort, suitable for all seasons.
  • Durable and quality passed down through generations.

True luxury should never come at the expense of the earth. We insist on sustainable production, with every product coming from and returning to nature.
